Geologic Map of the Petersburg National Battlefield and Adjacent Areas
Mapping the intersection of history and geology
Project Details
From 2014 to 2018, the Virginia Division of Geology and Mineral Resources (VA DGMR) mapped the geology in part or in total of five 7.5' quadrangles in and around Petersburg, Virginia. The goal was to produce a map for the National Park Service showing the geology of Petersburg National Battlefield and surrounding areas.
I provided both field support, GIS mapping, and GIS data management support beginning in Phase II of the project.
