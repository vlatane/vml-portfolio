The Superfund Redevelopment Geohub
One-stop shop for Superfund Redevelopment ArcGIS Online content
Project Details
Access the full Superfund Redevelopment hub site here.
EPA's Superfund Redvelopment Program (SRP) has used the ArcGIS Online platform for over five years. In 2021, development on a hub site began as an effort to organize SRP's content and provide a usable interface to the public for accessing Superfund data, Superfund site-specific storymaps, apps and more.
This hub site became known as "the SRP Geohub". I was on the development team for this project in a training capacity. My colleague showed me the ropes on hub sites and together we developed a site that is now one of SRP's most popular web apps. The Geohub's custom HTML cards guide the public through a library of content developed under the Superfund Redevelopment Program. The Geohub also functions as an open data site, where users can search for publicly-available Superfund GIS data.
