The Superfund Redevelopment Mapper
The "Zillow of Superfund Sites"
Project Details
Access the full Superfund Redevelopment Mapper application here.
EPA's redevelopment coordinators, land developers, community members and other stakeholders use "the Mapper" as an exploratory tool to view Superfund sites that may be fit for certain criteria for reuse, redevelopment or other land interests.
This web-based mapping tool helps stakeholders explore potential reuse opportunities at Superfund sites across the country. It provides users with Superfund site locations and options for adding data layers that highlight site features and site surroundings. I'm not the original author of this app, but I've maintained Superfund Redevelopment point data and curated other datasets powering the Mapper since January 2022. I helped implement version 2.0 and am part of the team that keeps the mapper running on a day-to-day basis. This is involves retrieving, cleaning and combining data from disparate sources, publishing data with minimal app downtime, staying up to date on the latest sources of authoritative data, and more.
